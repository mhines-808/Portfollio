About Me

Allen Hinds

UI/UX Designer & Front-end Developer

I design and build digital products that feel effortless to use. With six years of experience spanning product design, front-end development, and brand identity, I bring ideas from rough concept through to polished, shipped software.

My process is collaborative and research-driven. I care deeply about the space where user needs and business goals intersect — and finding the simplest, most considered path between the two.

Previously at Stripe and Linear, currently available for select freelance projects and collaborations.
